About Me

Hi there! I'm a soon-to-be Microbiology graduate (this November!), but honestly, my heart has always been split between the lab and the written word.

The truth about me: I write whatever my authenticity demands - poetry at 2 AM, prose that won't leave me alone, essays about random life observations. Consistency is overrated anyway.

This website is my little corner of the internet where I can be completely myself - no academic pretense, no trying to be "professional." Just words, thoughts, and whatever creative chaos is happening in my brain at any given moment.

My Substack readers never know what they're getting, and honestly, neither do I until I write it. That's the beauty of following your creative current!
