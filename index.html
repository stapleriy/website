I grew up reading everything I could find and somehow ended up with a BSc in Microbiology, which I have decided is just reading at a cellular level. I am now doing an MA in English Literature to back up my pretentious personality with a formal certificate. I am authentic like that.

I think there is something genuinely interesting at the intersection of science and story — the way both ask you to look very carefully at something and then describe what you find honestly. I am trying to do that. Most of the time. Sometimes I am just watching television.

I do not have a personal brand. I have a personality. There is a difference and I feel strongly about it.

Doraemon was a formative experience and I will not be taking questions. Also: all forms of art (to consume, not necessarily to produce). I love it all. Theatre, film, painting, someone's niche embroidery account at 2am. I am simply trying to be alive and sometimes that means crying at a well-composed shot.

I will recommend a show to you before I recommend a book. I have opinions about semicolons that are disproportionate to their importance. I think slowness is underrated. I am working on it.
