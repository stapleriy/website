I contain
multitudes.
(obviously.)

I grew up reading everything I could get my hands on and somehow ended up studying Microbiology — which, I have decided, is just reading at a cellular level. I am now doing an MA in English, which means I have simply switched the scale of the text I'm analysing.

I think there is something interesting at the intersection of science and story — the way both disciplines ask you to look very carefully at something and then describe what you find honestly. I am trying to do that. Most of the time.

I do not have a personal brand. I have a personality. There is a difference and I feel strongly about it.
