She Contains Multitudes.

(Obviously!)

The said multitudes being random ideas yearning to materialise. The same ones, that on second read morph into incoherent, nonsensical scribbles that puzzle her. She also, might or might not, contain multitudes of forced rhymes on most things she writes because her brain has categorised it as a personality trait.

The multitudes may extend to metaphors, that are pretentious of course (you know, to match her personality, or is it the other way around?). She enjoys art in multitude forms as well, if you aren't already tired of the word (to consume, not necessarily to create. but you already knew that). And, well, she grew up watching Doraemon.
