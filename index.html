I Contain Multitudes.

(Obviously!)

The said multitudes being random ideas yearning to materialise. The same ones, that on second read morph into incoherent, nonsensical scribbles that puzzle me. I also, might or might not contain multitudes of forced rhymes on most things I write because my brain has categorised it as a personality trait.

The multitudes may extend to metaphors, that are pretentious of course (you know, to match my personality, or is it the other way around?). I enjoy art in multitude forms as well, if you aren't already tired of the word (to consume, not necessarily to create. but you already knew that). And, well, I grew up watching Doraemon.
